Codex Capture Redesign V1

Guided Review Console

Design position: keep the proven PG v4 dark console and make Capture feel like a short guided review. The top stepper tells the user what to do, the center fields stay compact, and the right rail holds visual proof plus lock status. All action controls are rectangular. Pills are read-only status only.

Open calls: tooltip discovery uses dotted-underlined labels instead of extra question buttons; Stage vocabulary uses Draft / Ready / Locked / Replaced / Retired / Amended to avoid the HR-feeling word "Accepted"; empty optional sections collapse to reduce visual load.
