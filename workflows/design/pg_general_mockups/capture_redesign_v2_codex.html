Codex Capture Redesign V2

Decision Workbench

Design position: make Capture feel like reviewing one important document, with a health checklist that explains what is blocking versus merely recommended. This tests an always-visible checklist rail and a more document-like middle surface.

Open calls: tooltips use tiny helper notes inside the checklist rail; Stage uses Proposed / Current / Changed / Replaced / Retired to test a softer vocabulary; empty recommended sections remain visible with teaching placeholders.
